2. HealthKit Data

When you grant permission, the App reads health metrics from Apple HealthKit solely to display them inside the App and compute fitness insights. We do not use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing, and we do not share it with third parties. HealthKit data is processed on your device.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Most of your data — workouts, measurements, photos, and settings — is stored locally on your device. Some features require server storage:

  • Shared workouts you publish are stored in our cloud database (Google Firebase Firestore).
  • Safety features (blocked-user lists and content reports) are stored in Firestore to keep the shared community safe.
  • To enable these features, the App creates an anonymous account (Firebase Anonymous Authentication). No email or password is required.

4. AI Features

When you use AI Coach or AI generation features, the text you submit is sent to our secure backend (Google Cloud Functions) and processed by Google’s Vertex AI (Gemini) models to generate a response. Requests are protected by Firebase App Check. Please avoid submitting sensitive personal information in AI prompts.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, App Check) — Firebase Privacy
  • Google Cloud / Vertex AI (Gemini) — Google Cloud Privacy
  • Apple HealthKit — data governed by Apple’s on-device frameworks

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for tracking across apps or websites owned by other companies. Content you explicitly choose to share (e.g., shared workouts) becomes visible to other users of the App.

7. Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time from Settings → Delete Profile & Data (local data) and Settings → Account → Delete Account, which permanently removes your account and all associated server-side data (shared workouts, blocked list, and reports). This action cannot be undone.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can exercise deletion directly in the App, or contact us using the details below.
